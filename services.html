We Offer

Procurement services

We have partnered with small and medium-scale farmers in Kenya to try and promote organic farming for all the grains that we deal with. Most of our products are sourced locally and regionally hence empowering farmers and creating employment within the region.

  • we promote organic farming
  • products sourced locally and regionally
  • we empower farmers

Processing services

You’ll love working with us. For more than 2 years, our business has provided great service and superior product quality, while turning the Kenyan’s abundance into ingredients for the food.

Our innovative solutions improve process efficiency, product functionality and quality.

These solutions are backed by our experienced team of solvers, innovators, logistics experts and devoted customer service professionals.

Distribution services

We distribute our products either directly or through our approved partners, such as wholesalers, distributors
We have developed a third-party distribution framework that sets the standards for our distribution partners, including:
• Selection and due diligence to align with our partnering principles
• Minimum training requirements
• Remuneration and incentives and the identification and management of any potential conflicts of interest
• Distributor reporting, including distribution information, complaints and potential breaches and incidents
• Breach and consequence management.

Trading services

We also import and resell some of our food products.
